    About

    This app uses BlazePose and FaceMesh to detect your writing posture and face direction in real-time. The timer runs only when you are actively focused on studying.

    ⚠️ Important Study Guidelines

    • 1. Please make sure your face and both hands are fully visible in the camera frame for accurate detection.
    • 2. Maintain at least 25 cm (10 inches) distance from your copy or book. This helps both with detection and protects your eye health.
    • 3. Keep your phone or screen 40–50 cm from your face to reduce strain and avoid misdetection.

    Privacy Policy

    This app runs entirely in your browser. Your camera feed is never stored or sent anywhere. All posture detection happens locally, and your daily study time is saved securely in your browser's localStorage.
